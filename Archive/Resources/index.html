Automated Seismic Design and Analysis Platform (AutoSDA):

Overview of AutoSDA platform

AutoSDA platform is a Python-based computational developed by me. This platform automates the seismic design, nonlinear model construction, and seismic response simulation for steel moment resisting frames. The download link is here.

It has also been implement in EE-UQ (Earthquake Engineering with Uncertainty Quantification) developed by National Science Foundation Hazards Engineering Research Infrastructure (NHERI) SimCenter.

The detailed explanation for this platform could be found in this paper.
