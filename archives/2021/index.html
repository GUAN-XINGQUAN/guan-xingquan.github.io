Brief Introduction

Hi there!

My name is Xingquan Guan and I am a Ph.D. in Structural and Earthquake Engineering with minors in Statistics and Computer Science at the University of California, Los Angeles. My expertise is applying artificial intelligence in solving engineering problems. My current research is on developing the generalized seismic response simulation engine.

My research interests include:

  1. Application of artificial intelligence in enhancing community resilience
  2. Experimental test and finite element analysis of building structures under extreme loading
  3. Adoption of novel devices to improve seismic performance of conventional structures
  4. Development of advanced computational simulation platforms for design automation
